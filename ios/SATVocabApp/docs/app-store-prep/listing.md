## Full Description

Master 372 essential SAT vocabulary words with WordScholar -- a structured, game-based learning app designed for focused daily practice.

WordScholar uses a proven spaced repetition system to move words through five strength levels, ensuring long-term retention. Each day is split into a morning session (learn new words) and an evening session (review and reinforce), keeping study sessions short and effective.

HOW IT WORKS

- Flashcards introduce new words with definitions, example sentences, and SAT context
- Interactive games test your knowledge through multiple formats: fill-in-the-blank, image-to-word matching, quick recall, and real SAT-style multiple choice questions
- A 20-day adventure map guides you through four themed zones, unlocking new words as you progress
- Spaced repetition automatically schedules reviews for words you find difficult
- XP, streaks, and combo bonuses keep you motivated day after day

FEATURES

- 372 curated SAT vocabulary words with definitions, examples, and collocations
- Real SAT reading comprehension questions for in-context practice
- Word strength meter tracks each word across 5 mastery levels
- Morning and evening study sessions with smart scheduling
- Adventure map with 4 themed zones and 20 learning days
- Detailed stats: streaks, XP, word strength distribution, and stubborn word tracking
- Built-in pronunciation using text-to-speech
- Optional progress reports sent to a parent or tutor via email
- Local push notification reminders for morning and evening sessions
- No account required -- start learning immediately
- All data stored privately on your device

Whether you are preparing for the SAT, building your academic vocabulary, or just love learning new words, WordScholar makes daily practice a habit.
